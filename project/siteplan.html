Site Name:

velardevictuals.org

My background is very diverse, my family comes from Spain, France and Mexico mainly. My wife's family comes from Sweden, Scotland and England. Which makes my children even more diverse. We are a amalgamation of cultures and obviously this also reflects in our cuisine. The idea of this site is to provide recipes from our family's heritage so that we can share it with our loved ones, both family and friends.

Site purpose:

To introduce my children to their family's cuisine, and have a record of the recipes they love the most. Giving them access to them anywhere and anytime.
